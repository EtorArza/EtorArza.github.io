  • Comparing optimization algorithms in different machines

    Submitted to Theoretical Computer Science

    We develop a methodology to compare two algorithms in different machines that involves conservatively modifying the runtime of one of the algorithms and then using a modified version of the sign test, ensuring that the probability of type I error does not increase.
